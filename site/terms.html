8. Provenance Integrity

8a. Registration Is Not Proof of Ownership

Hash registration and metadata infusion record when a fingerprint was registered and by whom. They do NOT constitute proof of authorship, copyright ownership, or legal title. Registration creates a timestamped record, not a legal claim.

8b. Your Representations

By using register_hash, infuse_metadata, or any provenance tool, you represent and warrant that:

  • You are the creator or authorized representative of the work
  • You have the legal right to register provenance for the work
  • The metadata you provide (artist name, title, copyright holder) is accurate and not misleading
  • You are not attempting to claim provenance over a work created by someone else

8c. Fraudulent Registration

We reserve the right to:

  • Investigate suspected fraudulent provenance registrations
  • Flag, annotate, or remove registrations found to be fraudulent
  • Cooperate with legitimate rights holders who dispute a registration
  • Suspend or terminate accounts engaged in provenance fraud
  • Report fraudulent activity to law enforcement where appropriate

If you believe someone has fraudulently registered provenance for your work, contact legal@metavolve.com with evidence of your ownership.

8d. Dispute Resolution for Provenance Claims

In the event of conflicting provenance registrations, we will consider: timestamp priority (earlier registration), evidence of original creation, copyright registration, and any other relevant documentation. We are not an arbiter of ownership disputes and may refer parties to appropriate legal channels.
