Common Issues

Payment not applied

x402 payments are verified on-chain and may take a few seconds. If your credits weren't applied, include the transaction hash in your ticket below.

Tool returning errors

Check that your image is under 20MB and in a supported format (PNG, JPG, WebP). For print_ready, use product_size parameter (e.g., "a4", "poster_24x36").

Rate limited

Free tools: 10 calls/hour (anonymous) or 60/hour (registered wallet). Register a wallet at POST /api/wallet/register for higher limits and 10 free GCX credits.
